### 👤 Me

I am a reserved individual with a strong inclination toward reflection and personal development. I value ethical and moral principles, as well as education and courtesy in my interactions with others. Over time, I have developed the habit of seeking moments of solitude consciously—not as a form of withdrawal, but as a way to improve clarity of thought, reasoning, and creativity, especially after being in high-pressure environments.

I also have a particular appreciation for quiet moments, especially during the early hours of the morning, where I find an ideal space for reflection. The silence, fresh air, and calm atmosphere help me maintain balance and focus.

I grew up between Luxembourg and Portugal, completing my secondary education in Portugal. Throughout my journey, I have gained experience across different sectors, including hospitality, construction, volunteering, and technology, which has allowed me to develop a practical and adaptable mindset.

From an early age, I showed interest in areas such as the arts, mathematics, and technology. Over time, I explored different paths, which helped shape a logical way of thinking, combined with a personal and analytical perspective on the world and human behavior.

During my academic path, particularly in the final years of secondary education, I developed a deeper interest in subjects such as history, psychology, sociology, and philosophy, driven by a desire to better understand human behavior, society, and my own identity.

Throughout my development, I learned the importance of balancing focus and diversity of interests—avoiding both excessive dispersion and limitation to a single path—which I consider essential for effective and sustainable learning.

Currently, I am focused on building a balanced and sustainable life aligned with my values and personal goals. I am developing a career in IT, with a particular interest in remote opportunities that allow for greater efficiency, autonomy, and quality of life.

Looking ahead, I aim to establish a solid career in the technology field, continuously growing through experience while also dedicating time to personal projects that reflect my long-term goals and interests.
